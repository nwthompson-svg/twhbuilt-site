Topographic Survey Data

Georeferenced point data and CAD output — DXF and DWG — for planning applications, architect briefs, and drainage design. All data referenced to Ordnance Datum using OSTN15 transformation and delivered as multi-constellation RTK grade output across GPS, Galileo, GLONASS, and BeiDou. Ready to drop into your design model. Delivered within 48 hours.

Site Setting Out & Reference Points

Foundation lines, grid points, and finished floor levels supplied as coordinate data and physical marks on the ground. Available on a day rate or half-day basis — the resource you call when you need precision on site without a long-term commitment.

Ground Control Points for Drone Mapping

Surveyed GCPs for photogrammetry operators needing centimetre-accurate base data. Fast mobilisation, targets laid and measured, coordinates exported in your required format. Works with DJI, Autel, and all major RTK drone platforms.
